Sundays 8:30 & 10:30

Plan your visit now!

Who: Members & Non-members(open groups)
Group Size: 10-20 Adults
Group Dynamic: Anyone, co-ed or gender specific
Purpose: “Where friends become friendly”
Life Group Aspects: Conversation, fellowship, prayer, outreach, mutual care for one another
Time Frame: Meet Weekly for approx. 1hr
Leadership: One or Two leaders who nurture spiritual growth within the group who look for emerging leaders for future group multiplication.
Objectives: Biblical community engagement & group multiplication through fellowship, outreach, and service
Childcare: View children not as barriers to Life Groups, but as blessings. Be creative!

Life Groups provide both believers and unbelievers with the opportunity to engage in biblical community where they encounter Christ through fellowship, outreach and service.
