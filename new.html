What to Expect

You will find FAITH Church to be contemporary in its methods, conservative in doctrine and courageous in faith, as we strive tohelp and serve and meet the needs of our community.You can expect FAITH Church to Equip and Encourage . . . our members to “live their faith and share their lives” in their homes,their neighborhoods, their work place, and in their communities.You will experience FAITH Church as a place with a quality, warm, inviting Sunday morning worship service where people willfeel comfortable inviting a friend. By the sweet amazing grace of our Redeemer King, FAITH Church seeks to be a place where theHopeless find HOPE Helpless find HELP Lonely find a FRIEND Lost find a SAVIOR"


Membership Process

  • Step one, Attend a 3 week Discover Class (Understand who we are)
  • Step two, Fill out all membership paperwork and turn into Church office.
  • Step three, Meet with the Pastor (get to know you).
  • Step four, Introduced at a Family meeting for affirmation meets once a quarter. (Church gets to meet you).
