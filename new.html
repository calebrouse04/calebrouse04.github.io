What to Expect

Welcome to FAITH Church, where we embrace a contemporary approach, hold firmly to conservative doctrines, and fearlessly trust in our faith, which is rooted in the person and work of Jesus Christ. We are dedicated to equipping and inspiring our members to not only practice their faith, but also to share their lives with those around them – be it within their homes, neighborhoods, workplaces, or broader communities.


Planning your visit

What time is the Sunday service

Sunday morning services are offered at 8:30 and 10:30. The typical service lasts approximately 90 minutes.

What is the music style

We embrace a contemporary worship style, infusing modern musical elements that exalt the name of Jesus. Through engaging melodies and relevant lyrics, we provide an atmosphere that invites worshippers to connect with God in a meaningful way.

Where do I go and what should I wear?

In the front lobby, you will be welcomed by our hospitality team. They will be glad to assist you with questions and where to go from there. Most of our attendees dress casually and enjoy a few minutes of fellowship in the lobby, or outside on a nice day, before and after services.

Where do my kids go?

Classes for infants through fifth grade are offered during both services. Parents will check in their children at one of the stations located in the children’s area. You will receive a tag that is required to be shown to pick up your children at the end of service.
