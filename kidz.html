Whole Bible and Whole Truth

Faith Factory Kids Ministry welcomes children in kindergarten through 5th grade. We desire to come alongside families as they encourage their children towards pure, humble, and faith driven lives. We offer a loving environment with sound Bible teaching each week.

Sunday Mornings

8:30 | 10:30


On Sunday mornings, Faith Factory Kids enjoy a time of worship and teaching just for them! They begin each Sunday session together with fellowship and worship. Next, they break out into age-appropriate classes to dive into God’s Word through rich lessons, scripture memory, and activities that teach how to apply the Bible to their daily lives. They gather as a large group again for some final activities before dismissal.

Wednesday Nights

6:00-7:30pm


Wednesday nights are filled with music, fun games, and engaging activities as children learn the Bible basics in a relaxed setting. Our focus is on teaching Bible skills such as learning the books of the Bible, scripture reference drills, and more.
