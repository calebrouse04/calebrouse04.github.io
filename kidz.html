Scriptural, Relational, and Enjoyable

Faith Students is a place for 6th thru 12th graders to belong, grow, and thrive. The vision of Faith students is three fold; Scriptural, Relational, and Enjoyable. The first and main goal is to uphold God’s Word, to learn and grow to be more like Jesus Christ. Second is Relational. It is our hope that our students develop relationships with fellow students and adults leaders that have a positive impact on them for years to come. Finally, we desire Faith Students to be an enjoyable place which they look forward to being at and bringing their friends.

Wednesday Night


The main gatherings are Wednesday Nights from 6:00-7:30pm. These evenings include fellowship with other students, games to enjoy, group study of God’s Word and small group times for prayer and discussion. Some of the other activities in which Faith students participate include concerts, service projects, and special events like the annual Holiday Cook-off.
