Faith Kids welcomes children Kindergarten to Fifth Grade, partnering with families raising up Christs future generation of believers. With sound biblical teaching and a loving environment, we strive to encourage children towards pure, humble, and faith driven lives, while coming alongside families as we live out the Vision of Faith Church Loving-Learning-Living Like Jesus.

Sunday Mornings


Faith Kids uses Answers In Genesis (AIG) Bible Curriculum for our Sunday school program. The AIG curriculum provides a firm foundational teaching of God’s word, allowing the teachers and students to dive into rich content, scripture memory, and learn how to apply the Bible to their daily lives. Each week students are given a take home sheet to give parents insight as to what we are learning and tools to encourage continued application throughout the week. There are also great online resources available that can be utilized at home: answersingenesis.org. All K-5 th grade join together at the beginning and end of service to fellowship, and go into small groups for age appropriate teaching during lesson time.

Wednesday Night


Our students enjoy fellowship, music, and snacks. Since most of our students have been in a classroom much of the day, we try to keep this time a more relaxed setting with fun and engaging activities and games. Our emphasis for learning is on biblical knowledge and skills to help them learn bible basics such as memorizing the books of the bible and genres, scripture reference drills, etc. We also occasionally use our time Wednesday nights for special holiday events, crafts and activities.
